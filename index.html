Our Mission

The Northville High School Track and Field team is dedicated to inspire, challenge, and embolden the student-athletes of Northville High School to mature and grow academically, competitively, and benevolently. The mission of our track program is to provide opportunities for our student-athletes to develop skills which allow them to succeed as students, athletes, citizens, and leaders. NHSTF encourages our student-athletes to exemplify and demonstrate a winning work-ethic, positive sportsmanship, zeal for philanthropy, and respect for all others. We pride ourselves on our ability to develop our program which encourages participation from all ability levels, while providing a personalized learning environment for each event, through our close-knit, dedicated team of coaches and athletes.
