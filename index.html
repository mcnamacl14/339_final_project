Our Mission

The Northville High School Track and Field team is dedicated to inspire, challenge, and embolden the student-athletes of Northville High School to mature and grow academically, competitively, and benevolently. The mission of our track program is to provide opportunities for our student-athletes to develop skills which allow them to succeed as students, athletes, citizens, and leaders. NHSTF encourages our student-athletes to exemplify and demonstrate a winning work-ethic, positive sportsmanship, zeal for philanthropy, and respect for all others. We pride ourselves on our ability to develop our program which encourages participation from all ability levels, while providing a personalized learning environment for each event, through our close-knit, dedicated team of coaches and athletes. We are a track family, we are the Northville Mustangs.

Our History

Our Program was established in 1958 and was founded as a men's only program. We added the women's program in 1968 and ran the programs seperately. Starting in 1987 we switched to a combinded program. Since then, both the men's and women's programs have won multiple conference, regional, and state titles.

Our Goals

  • Teach young people the fundamentals of track and field
  • Make sports fun for the youth
  • Encourage and promote sportsmanship at a young age
  • Help high schoolers get active in a social way
